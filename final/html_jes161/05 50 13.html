<NPR>

 2.2.4.1 Surface Preparation 
<TXT>
Blast clean surfaces in accordance with 
<RID>
JIS Z 0310
</RID>
. Surfaces that will be exposed in spaces above ceiling or in attic spaces, crawl spaces, furred spaces, and chases may be cleaned by means of power tools. Wash cleaned surfaces which become contaminated with rust, dirt, oil, grease, or other contaminants with solvents until thoroughly clean. Steel to be embedded in concrete must be free of dirt and grease prior to embed. Do not paint or galvanize bearing surfaces, including contact surfaces within slip critical joints. Shop coat these surfaces with rust prevention.
</TXT>
