 1.4.1 Prevention of Corrosion 
<NTE>
 
<NPR>
NOTE: For all outdoor applications and all indoor applications in a harsh environment refer to Section 
<SRF>
09 96 00
</SRF>
 HIGH-PERFORMANCE COATINGS. High performance coatings are specified for all outdoor applications because ultraviolet radiation breaks down most standard coatings, causing a phenomena known as chalking, which is the first stage of the corrosion process. For additional information, contact The Coatings Industry Alliance, specific suppliers such as Keeler and Long and PPG, and NACE International (NACE).
</NPR>
 
</NTE>
 
<TXT>
